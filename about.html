About Me

I’m Mohamed Ali Suliman Salih, a Civil Engineer and certified Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) professional with a passion for creating safer, more resilient workplaces.

I hold a Diploma in Fire and Construction Safety Management, alongside international certifications such as NEBOSH, IOSH, OSHA, and the State Professional Certificate in Security Risk Management (STPM-C). My hands-on experience includes working with the Ministry of Infrastructure and Urban Development and Plan International Sudan, where I contributed to project planning, field supervision, and safety compliance.

My expertise focuses on HSE leadership, risk assessment, and safety supervision, ensuring that operations meet both local and international safety standards. I believe safety isn’t just about compliance — it’s about building a proactive culture of care, accountability, and trust.

I’m currently expanding my skills in Project Management and Occupational Safety Auditing through continuous learning programs such as PMP Essentials and ISO 45001 Lead Auditor training. My long-term goal is to grow into a senior role in HSE and Risk Management within large-scale infrastructure or humanitarian projects.

Outside of work, I enjoy learning about new technologies, improving leadership skills, and connecting with professionals who share a passion for safety and innovation.
